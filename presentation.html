A simple system to understand food

The NOVA System

Scientists created 4 groups to classify any food.
Once you learn this, you'll never shop the same way.

1 — Eat most 2 — Cooking 3 — Sometimes 4 — Rarely

The 4 NOVA Groups

1 · Unprocessed

As nature made it

🍎 Fruits🥬 Veggies🥚 Eggs🍗 Meat🍚 Rice

2 · Ingredients

For cooking, not alone

🫒 Oil🧈 Butter🧂 Salt🍯 Honey

3 · Processed

Real food + some extras

🧀 Cheese🍞 Bread🥫 Canned🥓 Ham

4 · Ultra-processed

Factory food. Addictive.

🥤 Soda🍟 Chips🍬 Candy🍕 Pizza

Ideal plate ratio

1

EAT MOST

Unprocessed & Minimally Processed

Nothing added, nothing removed. Washed, cut, frozen, cooked — but no chemicals.

🍎 Fresh fruits 🥬 Vegetables 🥚 Eggs 🍗 Meat & fish 🥛 Milk 🍚 Rice & pasta 🥜 Nuts & seeds 💧 Water & tea

→ Your plate should be mostly this.

4

EAT RARELY

Ultra-Processed Foods (UPF)

Industrial products from chemicals, additives & flavors you'd never have in your kitchen. Designed to make you eat more.

🥤 Soda & energy drinks 🍟 Chips & crisps 🍬 Candy & chocolate 🍕 Frozen pizza 🌭 Hot dogs & nuggets 🥣 Sweet cereals

→ Longer ingredients list = worse.

How to spot NOVA 4 on the label

🚩 Red flags

  • More than 5 ingredients
  • Words you can't pronounce
  • "Flavor", "color", "emulsifier"
  • Sugar in top 3 ingredients
  • Multiple sugars (glucose, fructose, maltose...)

✅ Green flags

  • Short list (1-4 ingredients)
  • Ingredients you recognize
  • Things your grandma would use
  • Sugar per 100g: under 5g
  • No "E-numbers" or chemical names
