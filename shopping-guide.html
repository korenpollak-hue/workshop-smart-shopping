🏷️ How to Read Labels

🔍
Sugar per 100g

LOW: under 5g · MEDIUM: 5-15g · HIGH: over 15g

📋
Ingredients list

Listed by amount (most → least). If sugar is in top 3: put it back!

⚖️
Serving size trick

Always compare per 100g, not "per serving". Companies use tiny servings to hide sugar.

⚠️
Secret sugar names

Glucose, fructose, maltose, dextrose, corn syrup, agave, maltodextrin, invert sugar — all sugar!

    💡 Rewe Tips

    🏷️ "Ja!" Brand

    Cheapest option for basics (pasta, rice, canned goods). Same quality, lower price.

    🌿 "REWE Bio"

    Organic option. Worth it for eggs and dairy. Not necessary for everything.

    📢 "Angebot" Signs

    Sales/deals — look for red or yellow price tags. Great for saving money!

    🥕 Loose > Packaged

    Buy loose vegetables instead of pre-packaged. Cheaper + less plastic.

    ⚖️ Price per kg

    Always check the small print on the price label — it shows price per kilo. That's the real comparison.
